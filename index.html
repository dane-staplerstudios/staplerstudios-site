Frequently asked questions
What is Stapler Studios?
Stapler Studios is an AI product studio that builds personalized software and modernizes workflows for B2B companies, with a focus on post-sales operations: customer success, onboarding, support, and product marketing. Products are built and proven in the studio, then deployed inside client operations and run jointly with client teams.
What does an AI product studio do?
An AI product studio designs, builds, and operates AI-powered software products rather than delivering advice or one-off projects. Stapler Studios builds its own products first, runs them in production, and then tailors and deploys them for clients. Every engagement starts from working software, not a blank page.
What is workflow modernization?
Workflow modernization means rebuilding how work actually flows through a team by replacing manual data assembly, reporting, and monitoring with AI agents that do the work while people review and approve. The result is the same operation with dramatically less manual effort and faster, more consistent output.
How is this different from buying a customer success platform like Gainsight or Planhat?
A customer success platform gives your team a dashboard your people must feed and operate. Stapler Studios deploys a system shaped to your workflows in which AI agents do the operating, health scoring, forecasting, reporting, and meeting preparation, with your team approving the output. It typically replaces the platform license, the implementation project, and the administrative headcount required to run it.
How long does a deployment take?
An AI Readiness Audit takes two to four weeks. Individual capability deployments take four to twelve weeks depending on scope. A full end-to-end deployment of the complete post-sales operating system is a phased program of four to six months, where each phase must be adopted before the next begins.
How are the AI agents governed?
Every agent runs under a trust-tier system with three levels: autonomous, fast-track, and requires-approval. Internal analysis can run autonomously; anything customer-facing routes through human approval. Every agent action is logged with its model, token usage, and cost, and every correction a team makes becomes an operating rule the system respects going forward.
Who does Stapler Studios work with?
Stapler Studios works with B2B software and recurring-revenue companies, frequently private-equity portfolio companies, that have dedicated customer success teams and want AI working in production rather than in pilots. Engagements are led by senior practitioners in customer success operations and AI engineering.
What does an engagement cost?
Engagements are priced against the systems and costs they replace, typically a platform license, its implementation, and the administration required to operate it, rather than billed hourly. The AI Readiness Audit establishes scope and a sequenced roadmap before any build investment is made.
